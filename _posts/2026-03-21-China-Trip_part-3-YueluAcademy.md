Our first day after meeting the group was to go visit Hunan University,
particularly Yuelu Academy, the oldest university in China.

Founded in 976AD, this university has been frequented by China's elite for
centuries. It's architecture and serenity has been kept up after rebuilding
from the Japanese bombing in WWII.
